Find your perfect work space before you leave.

WorkSpot shows you real-time seat availability, noise levels, and busyness for libraries and cafés near you — so you never show up to a packed room again. Every rating is logged by people actually there right now.

What data do we collect?

WorkSpot is community-powered — it only gets better when you log. Here's exactly what we use:

  • Your slider ratings — noise, busyness, air quality, light, and outlets
  • Which space you're at and the time of your log
  • Session duration from the timer
  • Location is only used while the app is open to show nearby spaces

We never sell your data. All ratings are anonymised before being shared publicly.
